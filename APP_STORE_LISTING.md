## Description (4000 chars max)

Never be surprised by a subscription charge again.

Billsy helps you track every subscription you pay for — Netflix, Spotify, iCloud, gym memberships, SaaS tools — all in one clean, simple app. Get reminded before renewals hit your account, see exactly how much you're spending, and spot subscriptions you've forgotten about.

**TRACK EVERYTHING**
• Add any subscription in seconds
• Supports all billing cycles — weekly, monthly, quarterly, yearly
• Track in any currency — INR, USD, EUR, GBP and more
• Mark subscriptions as free trials so you never forget to cancel

**STAY ON TOP OF RENEWALS**
• Calendar view shows every upcoming renewal at a glance
• Smart reminders — get notified 1, 3, 7, or 14 days before renewal
• See which subscriptions renew today, this week, or this month

**UNDERSTAND YOUR SPENDING**
• Monthly and yearly spend totals per currency
• 6-month spending trend chart
• Breakdown by category — Entertainment, Music, Productivity, Health and more
• Top subscriptions ranked by cost
• Spot unused subscriptions you're wasting money on

**CLEAN & MINIMAL DESIGN**
• No clutter, no ads, no sign-up required
• Works 100% offline — your data never leaves your phone
• Private and secure — stored locally on your device only

Download Billsy and take control of your subscriptions today.
